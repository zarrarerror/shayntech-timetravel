✦ Features

Everything You Need to
Control Database History

Snapshot, restore, audit, and validate — all from a single dashboard.

📸

Point-in-Time Snapshots

Capture your entire database state in milliseconds. Each snapshot is immutable and cryptographically signed.

⏪

Surgical Rollbacks

Roll back to any point in time with zero downtime. Only changed rows are restored — your latest writes stay safe.

🔗

Chain Validation

Every snapshot is linked via SHA-256 hashes into an auditable chain. Tamper-proof by design.

📊

Query History

Track every DDL and DML statement alongside your snapshots. See exactly what changed and when.

🔒

SOC 2 Compliance

Auto-generated compliance reports with signed audit trails. Ready for your next SOC 2 review.

🌐

Multi-Database

PostgreSQL, MySQL, MariaDB, SQL Server — manage all your databases from one place.

⚙️ How It Works

Three Steps to
Database Time Travel

No agents, no sidecars, no complex setup. Just connect and go.

1

Connect Your Database

Point TimeTravel at your existing database. We use logical replication — zero impact on production performance.

2

Snapshot on Demand

Trigger a snapshot from the dashboard, via API, or on a schedule. Each snapshot captures every row at that instant.

3

Restore & Validate

Browse snapshots, compare schemas, roll back with confidence. Every operation is logged and chain-validated.
